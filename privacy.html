Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the QuickBooks Integration application ("Application") developed by BV Tech Solutions, LLC ("Company," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects data.

1. Scope

This Application is an internal business tool used exclusively by authorized personnel of Bayview Solutions LLC, Cash Lane Holdings, and BV Tech Solutions, LLC. It is not a consumer-facing product.

2. Data We Access

Through Intuit's authorized OAuth 2.0 integration, the Application accesses the following QuickBooks Online data:

  • Financial reports (balance sheet, profit and loss, cash flow statements)
  • Company information and chart of accounts
  • Customer records, invoices, and journal entries
  • Transaction and remittance data

3. How We Use Data

All data accessed through this Application is used solely for:

  • Internal financial reporting and analysis
  • Remittance reconciliation with collection agencies
  • Invoice generation and posting
  • Cash flow forecasting and business planning

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth 2.0 credentials are stored securely using Google Cloud Secret Manager. The Application does not store QuickBooks financial data persistently — data is retrieved on demand from Intuit's API for real-time reporting. Access tokens are held in memory during active sessions and are not written to disk.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose QuickBooks data to any third parties. Data is accessed exclusively for the internal business purposes described above.

6. Data Retention

The Application does not maintain a separate database of QuickBooks data. Financial data resides in Intuit's QuickBooks Online platform and is subject to Intuit's data retention policies. OAuth tokens are retained only for the duration needed to maintain authorized access.

7. User Access

Access to the Application is limited to authorized employees and contractors. Authentication with QuickBooks Online requires explicit user consent through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our Application evolves. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact BV Tech Solutions, LLC at sean@bvs-llc.net.
